Emelio Mondares: From Basak Kagudoy

Emelio Mondares, hailing from the charming town of Basak Kagudoy in Lapu-Lapu City, has forged a remarkable path in the world of coding and web development.
Emelio's journey began at Marigondon High School, where their passion for technology ignited.

Early Passion for Technology:
Emelio's love for coding sparked during their high school years in Marigondon. They found joy in exploring the intricacies of web design and web development,
spending countless hours learning the art of creating websites.

College and Coding Adventures:
Emelio continued their education journey at the University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue (UCLM), where they pursued a degree in Computer Science.
College provided the perfect platform for Emelio to delve deeper into their coding passion. They eagerly embraced opportunities to work on web development projects, turning their passion into a promising career path.

Becoming a Software Engineer:
After graduating, Emelio's dream of becoming a software engineer came true. They secured their first job in the field and embarked on a journey of continuous learning and growth.
Emelio's dedication to coding and web development soon led them to excel in their role and gain recognition for their skills.

Coding Beyond the Workspace:
Emelio's love for coding extends beyond the office. In their free time, they immerse themselves in personal coding projects, always seeking to expand their horizons in the ever-evolving tech world.
Emelio's story is a testament to the power of passion and perseverance in the pursuit of one's dreams.
